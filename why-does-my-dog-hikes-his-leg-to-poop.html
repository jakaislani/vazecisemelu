Why does my dog hikes his leg to poop?

The higher a scent is placed, the better the chance the air flow will disperse it. This is believed to be the reason that male dogs lift their legs, in order to deposit scent as high as possible; not so much to “appear bigger” but to allow the scent to be better dispersed.

Why does my dog force himself to poop on walks?

Walking while pooping may be one way that your dog is able to leave their scent. The action of walking puts pressure on those scent glands, thereby leaving more of their musky personal signature for others to investigate. Why do dogs squat to poop?
