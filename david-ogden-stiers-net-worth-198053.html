David Ogden Stiers Net Worth

David Ogden Stiers was an American actor born on October 31, 1942 in Peoria, IL. He was best known for his role as Major Charles Emerson Winchester on the TV show M*A*S*H, and had a role in the Sci-Fi drama The Dead Zone. He also provided the voice of Jumba in Disney's Lilo & Stitch.
