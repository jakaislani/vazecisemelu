April and Phil Margera: TV Personalities with a Net Worth of $3 Million

April and Phil Margera are American television personalities known for their appearances on shows like “Viva La Bam,” “Jackass,” and “CKY videos.” Their son, Bam Margera, is a professional skateboarder, TV/radio personality, daredevil, and actor. The Margeras have a net worth of $3 million, earned through their various television appearances and as the parents of Bam Margera.

April Margera has also starred in several films, including “Minghags: The Movie” and “Bam’s Unholy Movie.” She has also published a cookbook called “April Cooks: There’s an Alligator in my Kitchen,” inspired by a skit in “Jackass” where her son put an alligator in her kitchen. In addition, April is a co-owner of “The Rose Hip Barn” in Thornton, Pennsylvania, which sells household products, purses, and refurbished furniture.

Phil Margera worked as a baker before his television career. He has also appeared on shows like “Celebrity Fit Club,” where he lost 41 pounds, and in the video game “Tony Hawk’s Underground 2” as part of the World Destruction Tour crew.

The Margeras also have another son, Jess Margera, who is the drummer for CKY.
