Breezango &amp; Swerve Overcome Imperiums Interference, Win Wild Street Fight

Breezango and Isaiah “Swerve” Scott teamed up tonight, opening up tonight’s special episode of NXT with a big win over Legado del Fantasma in a wild Street Fight. 

In a brutal street fight between two groups of superstars who have no love for each other, it was Isaiah Scott that helped end things, despite some interference from Imperium. Late in the match, Imperium came running down to ringside and began attacking Breezango, but both they and Legado del Fantasma were taken down when Isaiah Scott and Fandango jumped off of a forklift and knocked down all the superstars. After hitting his finisher on Santos Escobar, Scott pinned him for the win.

For more on how the match ended, check out below:

Santos and Co attack Breezango and Scott before they get to the ring. Wilde sets up a table as Mendoza tosses Scott into the barricade. Fandango crotches Santos on the barricade. In the ring, Scott dropkicks Mendoza into a chair. Mendoza hit the chair head first and falls out of the ring. Breeze blasts Wilde with a backstabber. Fandango puts a ladder on his head and spins around, taking out each member of LDF. Fandango slingshots Breeze into Wilde’s nuts. Scott surprises Santos with a running high knee Fandango pulls a fire extinguisher from under the ring and hands it to Breeze. Breeze sprays LDF with it. During the break, LDF hit triple basement dropkick into a chair on Breeze. Fandango is knocked off the apron and crashes through a table. IMPERIUM runs down to ringside and attacks Breezango. Scott and Fandango both dive off a forklift (yes, you read that correctly) and flatten LDF and IMPERIUM. Scott hits his finish on Santos for the win.
