September 2017: Brennan And Isabella Started Dating

Brennan Mulligan and Isabella Izzy Roland started dating publicly on September 15, 2017. At the time, Mulligan was working on his project, “Fallen For You” and “College Humor Originals.” The latter one is still running.

In the year, Mulligan didn’t make public posts about his dating life. However, on their second dating anniversary, he showered his Instagram wall with the couple’s pictures, alongside a lengthy caption about his life after Roland came into it.

He wrote,” Two years ago today, in a mind-boggling act of charity, Earth’s greatest human Isabella Natalie Francis Roland agreed to go out with me.”

He mentioned that his life since that day has been full of laughter and joy. Moreover, he lacks the words to describe how grateful he feels to know such a remarkable person.

Regarding their first post, the following year, on September 3, the lovebirds went to La Jolla Cove. Mulligan shared his girl’s picture with a seal on Instagram and tagged her. After that day, he often posted flaunting time spent together.

October 2018: The Lovebirds Went To Scotland For a Vacation

Apart from each other’s sense of humor, the lovebirds love to spend time together. Following that, Brennan and Isabella went to Scotland in October 2018 for a vacation.

The duo was there for a few days and visited Corran as well. Brennan shared some amazing pictures from their holidays on his Instagram wall. Next year, they walked together to Little Italy to enjoy a traditional Mulligan dinner.

Last year on January 31, Mulligan and Roland made their trip to Ireland. They visited the Cliffs of Moher, Fairy Bridges, Wishing Chairs, the Giant’s Causeway, and Dunluce Castle, and enjoyed a full Irish breakfast. They went to enjoy some traditional Irish music as well.

January 2021: Brennan Proposed To Isabella

Brennan and Isabella courted for over three years before he finally assembled the courage to propose to her.

On a fine day on January 21, Brennan kneeled in front of Isabella with a diamond ring. In response, Roland came with a silver Claddagh ring and put that on his ring finger.

The newly engaged flaunted their engagement rings on their official Instagram accounts.

September 2021: Brennan Announced Getting Married On Their Dating Anniversary

The romantic duo didn’t rush to decide on their relationship. On their fourth dating anniversary, Brennan announced they were getting married soon. However, he didn’t disclose the exact date.

He also expressed his love for Isabella and dedicated his Instagram update of the day to his fiance. He mentioned Isabella Izzy Roland as the best thing that ever happened to him and praised her as the most brilliant, beautiful, talented, kindest, and funniest person he had ever met.

With tons of Roland’s pictures, he wrote: “We’re Getting Married!” On their dating anniversary the following year, Mulligan addressed Isabella as his soon-to-be wife in an Instagram post.

Fans will soon get to meet Isabella as Brennan Lee Mulligan wife. The duo might surprise their fans with their big-day snaps shortly.
