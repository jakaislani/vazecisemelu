Rhea Ripley Tells Roman Reigns To Acknowledge Her After WWE RAW

Rhea Ripley has worked very hard to establish herself as one of the top female Superstars in WWE. In fact, she has seen a lot of success in the company, being a multi-time champion. Ripley is never afraid of any WWE Superstar, whether that be male or female. She had an intense confrontation with Solo Sikoa on RAW this week. Now it seems she decided to mock Roman Reigns after the show ended.

Click here for our full coverage of WWE RAW.

The Eradicator is having a great time as part of The Judgment Day faction. She has always been a constant thorn in the side of any faction she has been feuding with, whether it be The O.C. or currently, The Bloodline.

As seen on this week’s episode of Monday Night RAW, Rhea Ripley had an intense confrontation with Bloodline member Solo Sikoa. This was preceded by Dominik Mysterio cutting a promo on The Bloodline. Just when Sikoa tried to get in Mysterio’s face, Ripley intervened.

Following the conclusion of Monday Night RAW, The Eradicator took to Twitter and used Roman Reigns’ signature catchphrase, as a way to mock him as well as The Bloodline.

Acknowledge me.

The Judgment Day will challenge The Usos for the RAW Tag Team Championships next week on the 30th Anniversary of Monday Night RAW. We’ll have to see how Rhea Ripley will be involved in the match.
