Nigel Gaisie Raped And Caused Ebony Reigns Accident-Kennedy Agyapong Alleges(Video)

The controversial politician representing Assin-Central, Hon. Kennedy Agyapong has dropped another bombshell against the general overseer of the Prophetic Hills Chapel, Prophet Nigel Gaisie over the death of the late Ebony Reigns.

The maverick politician who has taken it upon himself to expose the malicious acts of some prophets in Ghana has alleged that Nigel Gaisie raped and killed the late Ebony Reigns because he had some issues with her.

He claimed that when Ebony decided to come public and expose Nigel Gaisie for all his evil deeds, Nigel Gaisie staged her death through the fatal accident which killed her best friend along with her bodyguard.

He also said that Nigel Gaisie has been sleeping with a lot of ladies, some who are even older than him and threatened to mention the names of all those ladies.

Speaking on Net2 TV, Kennedy Agyapong expresses his disappointment in the Christian Council in Ghana for looking unconcern while these so-called men of God deceive and harm innocent people.

He further added that he will not mention the names of these ladies the prophet has been sleeping with because one of them pleaded with him not to do that, adding that the person threatened to commit suicide if her name is being mentioned on TV.
