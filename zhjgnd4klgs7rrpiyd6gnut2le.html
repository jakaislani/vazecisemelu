Body found on Omaha beach - New Zealand News

The man's body was found at Omaha after he took his kayak out fishing and failed to return this morning. Photo/ Google

The body of a 79-year-old fisherman has been found at Omaha beach after he was reported missing this morning.

Police were told the man, who had taken his kayak to go out fishing at Omaha, had not returned about 10.50 this morning.

Police conducted a search including using the Eagle helicopter and found the body floating in the water.

The man was wearing a lifejacket and weather conditions were calm.
