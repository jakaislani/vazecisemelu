SheBecame Victoria's Secret Angel In 2019 And Walked On Four Victoria's Secret Fashion Shows

British model Leomie Anderson is a 'Victoria's Secret Angel' who made her way to the section in April 2019 after nine years of modeling credits. She has appeared in four of Victoria's Secret Fashion Shows and Ready to Wear – Spring / Summer 2011 Fashion Show (2011).

Moreover, starring in Fenty Beauty campaign (2017), Monster Management S/S 15 Models Film: Ill (2014), and Asylum - A Monster Film (2014) comes forth along with her modeling career.

Leomie Became Victoria's Secret Angel In 2019

Leomie Anderson, who started modeling from a young age of fourteen, ramp walked in several events and shows, including four Victoria's Secret Fashion Shows back to back.

Later, on the 4th of April 2019, the Victoria's Secret announced that the 27-year-old British model became the new Victoria's Secret Angel. Concerning the news, the British model shared her excitement on Instagram:

"Mate I am still in shock... this is something I was even scared to dream about because I never thought it would come true but I am insanely excited to announce that I am Victoria's Secret's newest ANGEL!! I can't even believe it..."

Besides her modeling career, she is widely known for her outspoken voice and has her brand named LAPP, the Brand. The platform allows girls to share their views, which also is a collection of streetwear pieces of bras to tracksuits.

She Walked On Four Victoria's Secret Fashion Shows

As a model, ramp walk comes as a normal thing to do, but being a part of Victoria's Fashion Shows gives a far good vibe. Leomie Anderson, the British model, has walked on four Victoria's Secret Fashion Shows for five years.

She walked in Segment 1: Boho Psychedelic on her third attempt in 2015. After a year, she walked in Segment 2: Mountain Romance and Segment 4: Secret Angel in 2016. Leomie also was part of Segment 1: Punk Angels and Segment 5: Nomadic Adventure in 2017 and Segment 5: Mary K X VS in the following year in 2018.

Leomie Anderson at Paris Fashion Show (©: Instagram/Leomie Anderson)

Leomie first appeared as a model in 2011 in Ready to Wear – Spring / Summer 2011 Fashion Show. She made her debut ramp walk for Trina Turk, Guli, Marc by Marc Jacobs, Topshop Unique, and Emanuel Ungaro.

Throughout her career, she got featured in many campaigns such as Fenty Beauty and Pat McGrath's beauty line.
