Iheoma Amanda Nnadi (born 15 May 1995) is an Igbo Nigerian beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Most Beautiful Girl in Nigeria 2014. She represented Akwa Ibom state and represented Nigeria in the Miss World 2014 contest but Unplaced.

Personal Life

Iheoma is married to footballer Emmanuel Emenike. The couple welcomed their 1st child in August 2017. They had their 2nd child on 13 March 2020.
